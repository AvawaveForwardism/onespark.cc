"Trans Rights" or Trans Liberation? A Collection of Arguments

 

- rights won in a bourgeois society can be rolled back any time like with workers rights or other minority rights as has happened in the past and as is happening now

 

- transphobia, like racism, emrges from bourgeois society as a means of redirecting worker and oppressed thinking away from class consciousness (fascism being the most extreme example on this)

 

- escalation of transphobia and other forms of reactionary thought are evidence of a decaying system which should embolden us 

 

- the movement for the liberation of trans people needs to break from liberal forms of thinking and needs to be reorganized along more radical lines at the very least, ideally marxist partically maoist.

 

- the trans liberation movement also needs to become linked up with the movement to overthrow capitalism, colonialism, and the patriachal ideology birthed from those systems

 

- the trans liberation movement should move to become more centralized, as in more cooperation between diffrent groups within countries and globally

 

- so called "conservatives" should be denounced for what they are reactionary liberals not fascists or "conservatives". They fundamentally follow the ideology of liberalism and haven't broke from and aren't working to "conserve" anything but go backward, regress, and upend the current state of things in a regressive direction.
