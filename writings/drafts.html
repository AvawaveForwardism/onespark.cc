Brief Explanation

Here is a list of documents which are planned to be written but are to embryonic in planning drafting to have a dedicated draft page with fleshed out details and references.

Marxism Applied to Transition

there is nothing to display yet

Documents on Marxism-Leninism-Maoism

1 - Against the Strawman

Criticism against the mischaracterization of Marxism-Leninism-Maoism by revisionists of all stripes and those within the maoist camp with subpar ideological development (mainly those who are chronically online)

2 - Economic Computerization and Maoist Economics

An investigaiton of computerization under a socialist economy following maoist principles.

Documents on HRT & HRT Labs

1 - HRT Labs, DIY, and HRT Under Socialism, A Marxist Take (preliminary title)

Drafting largely spurred on in response to this paper. Prompting an analysis of DIY HRT labs from a Marxist-Leninist-Maoist perspective as well as investigating what hrt, and transition more broadly, could look like under socialism.

Polemical Writings

1 - Where is Azania?

Investigation and Definition of the national bounds of Azania, the need for a unified socialist state in east Africa, and against the thieving of the name "Azania" by South Africans.

2 - The Correct Lyrics of The Internationale

A brief criticism of the prevelance of revisionist translations of the internationale and what better lyrics would instead be

3 - Political and Stylistic Criticisms of the Dominant Style of Edits (preliminary title)

Appraisal of the domniant styles prevelant in "edits" or other agitational media for the socialist cause.

CRITICISMS

All critiques can be directed to my email AvawaveForward@proton.me. I accept criticisms from all well meaning and serious people, only through struggle can higher understanding be developed.
