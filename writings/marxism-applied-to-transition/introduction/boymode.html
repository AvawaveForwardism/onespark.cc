Current Title: "What is the Boymode?"

Subject 1 - "A Necessary Stage"

Here I will define what the boymode is and what role it plays in transition (based on my experience, which is the case for all of this first part of this series).

In the outline document I wrote:

"defining the boymode and talking abt how the boymode forms an integral component of transition and so should be upheld for a time, drastically varying depending on one's situation of course."

Subject 2 - "For Progress or Reaction?"

In this subject I intend to discuss the dual character of the boymode how it can be, again based on my experience, utilized for progressive aims while simultaneously be a force to throw transition into retrograde.

In the outline for this document I wrote:

"defining the boymode as having a dual character, it can for a time serve to protect the advancement of transition but if it reaches the point of holding back the completion of transition it transforms into its opposite and becomes reactionary. emphasizing the need to question "for what aim" when boymoding, ie whether it is done for protection and to maintain transition or something used to hold back transition."
