IN DEFENSE OF TRANSITION

Ideologically Unshackling the Transgender Liberation Struggle

Current Thesis Rough Draft

At present this document’s primary aim is providing a thorough critique and repudiation of the dominance of reactionary, postmodernist, liberal, anarchist, and all other bourgeois ideological currents within and without the transgender liberation movement which hurl vile attacks against us transgender people. Advocating and defending the necessity of revolution for the complete liberation of trans people. Developing this argument on ideological and scientific grounds:

1 - A defense and explanation of the existence of trans people medically and historically. Demonstrating how one’s sex is transformed in reality making them that of their identified sex

2 – Repudiating ideologically bankrupt idealism of placing identification above all and ignoring concrete realities

3 – Defending transgender people’s position as an oppressed group within society

4 – Exposition of the myriad of differing tendencies within and without the trans liberation struggle (main focus on repudiating reformism)

5 – Defense of the necessity of grasping Marxism-Leninism-Maoism to achieve liberation via revolution. That is explaining how transgender liberation will only come out of the barrel of a gun with the conquest of power by the proletariat nationally and internationally.

NOTE I refer here mainly to binary transgender people, however the existence of non-binary/3rd gender identies must and will certainly be defended within this document. It is not the focus of this document however and I am not knowledgable on the subject, if you are non-binary and/or have some readings which you would like to reccomend on this subject to help improve the factual accuracy and ideological sharpness of this document then please forward them to my email. Thank you!

CRITICISMS

All critiques can be directed to my email AvawaveForward@proton.me. I accept criticisms from all well meaning and serious people, only through struggle can higher understanding be developed.
