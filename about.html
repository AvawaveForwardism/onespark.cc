About Me and The Site

A Website in Service of Revolution?

I've created, or rather overhauled, this site with the conscious understanding that it should serve revolution. It is a Marxist-Leninist-Maoist principle to vigourously strive to contribute to revolution in all ways one can. Previously I had no idea what I could do to contribute to revolution. I am isolated irl and so can't contribute in that realm and before I hadn't any clue what to do with my life. I then realized that something we are in need of today is more people advocating for marxist perspectives on all things there are. I haven't, thus far, found anyone going in any depth to advocate for a marxist perspective on how to navigate gender transition, east African history, or computer science (though I'm sure there is someone out there espousing genuine marxist ideas on computer science, it is a broad field afterall). And so I construct this website as, alongside other things, a means of propagating a marxist perspective on these specific topics.

I must honestly declare, however, that in the grand scheme of things I am not that well read or knowledgable on marxism or computer science. I started coding only relatively recently and only started studying marxism in any serious and vigourous capacity 2 years ago. So while I may not be the most ideologically sharp I try to make up for this in my enthusiasm to learn and self-criticize. I genuinely wish to see the liberation of all people's which I know will not occur in my lifetime. And so I make this website in service of the noble goal of communism under the guidance of the revolutionary proletarian science of Marxism-Leninism-Maoism!

Who is "AvawaveForwardism"?

"AvawaveForwardism", or just Ava, is the owner and operator of this webiste. I am a young trans woman currently living in Japan of completely Azanian (east African) descent. I am currently in the third year of my transition, however due to extreme circumstance I remain a "hardcore boymoder" which is to say that my transition has been severely stunted as a result of forces beyond my control (tho I am working to rectify this).

I am most interested and passionate about Marxism-Leninism-Maoism, computer science, east African history, and Marxism-Leninism-Maoism applied to transition. I also make art which you can find all my public works here

"Red-Girlmoderism", "Anti-Boymoderism"? What Are These Things?

"Red-Girlmoderism" and "Anti-Boymoderism" are essentially 2 different terms intending to mean the same thing. I don't think it is appropriate to get into why I've coined 2 terms for the same concept, but essentially Red-Girlmoderism and Anti-Boymoderism are, is, the general idelogical framework I've developed in regard to the application of Marxism-Leninism-Maoism to gender transition. That is the most concise definition I can possibly give.

I suppose it is also important to declare that I don't coin these terms as a means of differentiating them from Marxism-Leninism-Maoism, it is simply Marxism-Leninism-Maoism applied to the particular problems and contradictions of gender transition. Or rather even more specifically my transition as I haven't done the work to generalize the concept quite yet to be properly applicable to others.
