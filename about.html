About The Site

A Website for What Purpose?

This website was created with the primary aim of contributing to the struggle of repudiating bourgeois ideas on 2 issues. Firstly the transgender question and secondly the east African liberation struggle. The current stranglehold of bourgeois ideology within both these struggles is suffocating. Redirecting the rage and energy of the oppressed into politics and actions which continue to perpetuate our subjugation.

If we oppressed people are to liberate ourselves we must utilize correct ideas, which Marxism-Leninism-Maoism provides. From this, here are written articles and resources aggregated for the repudiation of bourgeois ideology so that we can strive toward genuine liberation.

Who is "AvawaveForwardism"?

"AvawaveForwardism", or just Ava, is the owner and operator of this webiste. I am a young trans woman currently living in Japan of completely Azanian (east African) descent. I am currently in the third year of my transition.

I am most interested and passionate about Marxism-Leninism-Maoism, computer science, and east African history.
