Explanation of Purpose

Hello this page was made as a supporting resource of this guide on producing your own transdermal (topical) hrt. This calculator is made to take some of the burden certain aspects of the calculation process of making your solution.

Inspired largely by this recipe customizer on hrt.mom, though the topical guide on hrt.mom is incredibly rudimentary. That being said, here you can customize the kind of of hrt (masculinizing or feminizing) you are making, the volume, concentration based on how much your container dispells, and what resources you have available to make your hrt. Additionally, the fundamental ratio's of the fluids is based on TeaHRT/Open Gate Labs' recipe. (referenced in the guide)

NOTE: in the input boxes only put in numbers and no other symbols AND select the ingredients you have available, the calculator adjusts based on that
