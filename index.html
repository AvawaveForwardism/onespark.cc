Marxism Applied to Transition

The trans liberation movement has generally been captured by bourgeois ideologues, notably liberals and post-modernists, leading the movement to be channeled into reformist politics and the limitation of the struggle to merely the “rights” of trans people all the while our situation is becoming more precarious day by day and the bourgeois states worldwide are espousing ever more reactionary transphobic rhetoric. Which in turn exposes the hollow pursuit of only rights for trans people within bourgeois society.

In such a situation it is imperative that us trans people unite under the only ideology, Marxism-Leninism-Maoism, which can deliver complete liberation. On this basis, this series of documents is drafted to take part in the struggle to repudiate the varying bourgeois views strangling the trans liberation movement.

"There is no social problem that man cannot solve. The more we apply the principles of Marxism-Leninism-Maoism, the more we will find solutions to new problems in the service of the international proletariat and the peoples of the world. Thus, the political, economic, organizational and military problems we face in the course of the class struggle can be properly solved if we start from the world outlook of the proletariat."

- Communist Party of Brazil (Red Fraction), "Unite Under Maoism"
