Marxism Applied To Transition

A Series

My transition has endured many ups and downs, and thru it I've neared death more than once. The difficulties I've faced in my transition, thus far, have been rather immense. As a result I've turned to the framework which the revolutionary universal proletarian science Marxism-Leninism-Maoism provides in order to, again thus far, successfully navigate the contradictions of my transition. In a world where it is becoming ever more difficult and dangerous for one to transition I think it is crucial to propagate a path of gender transition which was forged in the most dangerous of struggles. It has helped me thus far and I desire to help other people, so I will try my best to make and provide marxist arguments and perspectives on how to navigate the diffculties of transition successfully.

This has been my primary point of study for the past 2 years so I hope I can communicate this well to whoever reads this. The most recent installments of the series will be linked here when I begin writing.
